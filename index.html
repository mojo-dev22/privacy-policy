Privacy Policy for Decision God

Last Updated: March 9, 2026
Key Point: Decision God is a completely offline application that does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. This app operates entirely on your device without requiring internet connectivity.

1. Information Collection and Use

Decision God does not collect any personal information. The app is designed to function completely offline and does not:

  • Collect personal data of any kind
  • Access your contacts, location, or other device information
  • Require user registration or login
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Connect to the internet or external servers
  • Store information in the cloud

2. Data Storage

All data created within Decision God is stored locally on your device only:

  • Questions and Answers: Your decision history is stored locally on your device using Android's internal storage
  • App Settings: Your preferences (theme, settings) are saved locally using Android DataStore
  • Favorites: Marked favorite decisions are stored locally on your device

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

3. Data Sharing

Decision God does not share any data because we do not collect or have access to any data. The only sharing functionality is:

  • Manual Sharing: When you choose to share a decision result using Android's built-in sharing feature, this is handled entirely by your device's operating system and the app you choose to share with

4. Internet Permissions

Decision God does not request internet permissions and cannot access the internet. This ensures your privacy and demonstrates our commitment to keeping the app completely offline.

5. Third-Party Services

Decision God does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics services
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud storage services

6. Children's Privacy

Decision God is designed to be family-friendly and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any data, we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

7. Security

Since Decision God operates completely offline and does not collect or transmit personal information, there are no security risks related to data transmission or storage on external servers. All app data remains securely on your device under your control.

8. Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Individual Entries: Delete specific decision entries through the app's history feature
  • All Data: Use the "Reset App" feature in settings to delete all locally stored data
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app will remove all associated data from your device

9. International Data Transfers

Since Decision God does not collect or transmit any data, there are no international data transfers of personal information.

10. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we do not process personal data as the app functions entirely offline without data collection.

11. Your Rights

Although we do not collect personal data, you have complete control over any data created within the app on your device. You can:

  • View all stored decision history within the app
  • Delete individual entries
  • Clear all data using the reset function
  • Uninstall the app to remove all data

12. Entertainment Purpose Disclaimer

Decision God is designed for entertainment purposes only. The app provides decision-making assistance for fun and creative thinking. It should not be used as a substitute for professional advice in medical, legal, financial, or other serious matters.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page. Since the app is offline, you will see privacy policy updates through app updates distributed via the Google Play Store.

14. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the Decision God app, you can contact us through the Google Play Store developer contact information.

15. Regional Compliance

This privacy policy is designed to comply with privacy laws worldwide, including:

  • GDPR (European Union)
  • CCPA (California, USA)
  • PIPEDA (Canada)
  • Privacy Act (Australia)
  • LGPD (Brazil)
  • Other applicable privacy regulations
Summary: Decision God respects your privacy by not collecting any personal information whatsoever. The app functions completely offline, ensuring your decision-making process remains private and secure on your device.
